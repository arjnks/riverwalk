Cursor Trail &

The custom cursor has two layers active at all times. A gold dot marks the precise cursor position. Around it, a circular ring fills clockwise as you scroll — your position on the page made visible. Behind the cursor, 10 trailing dots follow with cascading lag, creating a comet-tail effect.

Line-by-Line
Text Reveal

Each line of text is clipped inside an overflow:hidden container. When the block scrolls into view, every line independently slides upward into position with staggered timing — 100ms between each. Far more editorial than a block fade.

3D Card Tilt
