The Grand Ballroom

Scale That Commands Awe

At 18,000 square feet, our Grand Ballroom is the crown jewel of Aura. Soaring 32-foot ceilings. Integrated 4K projection. A sound system engineered by the same firm that built the Vienna Philharmonic Hall. For up to 1,200 seated guests, the Ballroom redefines what "grand" means.

The Experience

Every Detail. Engineered.

From the moment your guests step through the doors of Aura, every sensory detail — sight, sound, scent, temperature — is orchestrated to perfection.

01

Acoustic Architecture

Engineered for flawless sound at any volume. Our halls use variable acoustic panels that adapt in real-time to speech, live music, and cinematic audio.

02

Intelligent DMX Lighting

Over 3,400 individually controlled LEDs and moving heads. Set the scene from intimate candlelight to full theatrical spectacle with a single command.

03

Culinary Excellence

Our 12,000 sq ft kitchen operates at Michelin-grade standard. Capacity for 2,500 plated covers simultaneously. Catering to Kerala, Continental, and beyond.

04

Hydraulic Staging

Our signature hydraulic stage can rise, rotate, and reconfigure in under 90 seconds. Transforming a conference setup into a concert stage without the crowd noticing.

05

Bridal & VIP Suites

Four dedicated suites with private entrances. Private vanity rooms, champagne service, and direct stage access. Designed for the people at the centre of it all.

06

Concierge Planning

Your dedicated Aura concierge is available 24/7 from the moment you book to the last guest's departure. We don't just host events — we orchestrate experiences.
